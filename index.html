A Mac and Cheese Recipe

For when You're Trying to be Really Quiet at Night

A Meal for surviving the Darkness

Hello, and welcome to my recipe for Microwave Macaroni and Cheese. To tell the truth, it's simply an edit of the suggested instructions from the box of the standard Kraft Macaroni and Cheese instructions, modified for a microwave, with other changes. Would this truly be considered a "Recipe"? Probably not, but it should hopefully be enough to satisfy a hungry soul in the dead of night, so I personally belive that it's worth sharing.

Ingredients

  • One box of Standard Kraft Macaroni and Cheese (Other brands should suffice)
  • One Bowl that can safely contain it
  • Four tablespoons of Salted Butter
  • Garlic Powder (at least a pinch or so)
  • One Fork

Instructions

  1. Pour Macaroni into bowl and set aside the included Cheese Powder. Fill bowl with Water until all Macaroni is Submerged. There should not be much space between the surface of the water and the macaroni itself. Put in a Microwave for Two minutes and Thirty Seconds.
  2. After extracting the bowl from the microwave, stir it around with your Fork until it's no longer lumped together. Return it to the microwave for Two additional minutes.
  3. While the Mac is cooking, use this time to gather the other ingredients and defeat any enemies that may want to eat it first.
  4. Once the Macaroni is finished, add the four tablespoons of butter. Stir until the butter has fully melted.
  5. Add included Cheese Powder and stir. Once mixed, add Garlic Powder. Only a small amount is necessary, as adding too much will make the garlic taste too powerful.
